Ingredients:
- 1 Tin Cornbeef
- 1 Bottle of ketchup
- 2 slices of bread
- 1 Tomato
- 1 Lettuce

Directions:
	1. Place cornbeef in a plate, and use a fork to crush and spread it out onto the plate.
	2. Pour ketchup to cover about 1/5 of the corn beef
	3. Use fork to mix ketchup into cornbeef
	4. Place cornbeef onto both slices of bread
	5. Strip pieces of lettuce and place on one side, and cut tomato slices and place them on the other side.
	6. Close sandwhich and place in toaster at 250 degrees F for two minutes.
